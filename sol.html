Solutions

How can both sides come to a compromise? Some ideas for both sides to come to a compromise are:
  • Improve drug testing to get into the facilities
  • Provide the drugs there to make sure that they are untainted
  • Try and appeal to federal government to loosen restrictions on SISs so that they aren't illegal
  • Put them in neighborhoods and communities where drug abuse is a bigger problem
